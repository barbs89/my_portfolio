About {

Born in Chile, Barbara moved to Australia in 1993 with her family. Ever since, Barbara has built a life in the wonderful city of Melbourne.

At aged 28, she is busy pursuing a new career as a Web Developer.
Balancing work commitments within the retail world and studying at Coder Academy, she is focused on an exciting new chapter in her life.

Formally educated as a Graphic Designer at Tractor Design School, she is able to apply constantly using her talents to pursue personal projects which allows her to be aware of the latest developments in contemporary design.
Life outside of work for Barbara revolves around her interests in camping, fitness and travelling. Naturally, Illustrating and Animation are an important focal point of Barbara’s life.

Barbara’s love of design and technology has led her to develop a passion for Front-End Web Development.

Barbara’s life goals are to become a citizen of the world and reach the stage in her career where she can live as a Digital Nomad.

To reach that stage, she would like to gain experience with employers who are willing to nurture her talents and help her develop as a programmer. In return, her commitment and drive would be a great asset for any organisation.

The ultimate dream for Barbara upon graduation is to be employed by a tech company.
Any company employing Barbara would be lucky to have an employee with such fresh ideas and skills in design.
